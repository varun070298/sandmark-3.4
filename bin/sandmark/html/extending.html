Extending SandMark

SandMark is designed to be simple to extend. A plugin-style architecture makes is very easy to add new watermarking, obfuscation, and code optimization algorithms. Additionally, infrastructure is in place to construct
  • inheritance graphs,
  • interference graphs,
  • control flow graphs, and
  • call graphs;
to perform various static analyzes, such as
  • stack-simulation,
  • liveness, and
  • defuse;
and a large number of graph algorithms, including graph visualization.

This information is currently sketchy but will be improved in future releases of SandMark. In the mean time, please consult the on-line APIs at http://sandmark.cs.arizona.edu/API/3.4.0/index.html.
